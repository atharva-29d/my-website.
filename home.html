VELOCE RACING

Veloce Racing is a Formula Student team that participates in India's largest Engineering Design Competition. Established in 2011 by a passionate group of motorsport enthusiasts, Veloce Racing represents Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune. After 12 years of building a strong legacy in the combustion category and achieving a world rank of 2, the team is now embarking on an exciting transition to the electric vehicle category. The main objective remains to design and construct a vehicle that excels both nationally and internationally, reflecting the unwavering commitment and 'NEVER GIVE UP' attitude
